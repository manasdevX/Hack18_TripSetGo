# Manali Travel Guide

## Overview
Manali is a high-altitude hill station in Himachal Pradesh, India. Located at 2,050m altitude. Gateway to Leh-Ladakh via Rohtang Pass (open May–October). Best time to visit: October–June for adventure; December–February for snow.

## Top Hotels

### The Himalayan (5-star, Old Manali)
- Price: ₹8,000–₹15,000 per night
- Rating: 4.7/5
- Amenities: Spa, mountain views, fireplace rooms, restaurant with Himachali cuisine.

### Apple Country Resort (4-star, Manali)
- Price: ₹4,000–₹7,000 per night
- Rating: 4.4/5
- Amenities: Garden setting, apple orchards, mountain views, restaurant.

### Snow Valley Resorts (3-star, Manali)
- Price: ₹2,500–₹4,500 per night
- Rating: 4.1/5
- Amenities: Heated rooms, restaurant, close to mall road.

### Zostel Manali (Hostel, Old Manali Road)
- Price: ₹500–₹900/night (dorm), ₹1,800–₹3,000 (private)
- Rating: 4.3/5
- Social hostel with rooftop, popular with backpackers.

### Hotel John Banon (Budget, Mall Road)
- Price: ₹1,200–₹2,500 per night
- Rating: 3.9/5
- Clean rooms, central location, mountain views.

## Top Attractions

- **Rohtang Pass** (3,978m): Snow activities (May–October), permit required ₹600/person. Entry permit via District Collector office or HRTC.
- **Solang Valley**: Skiing in winter, zorbing and paragliding in summer. Paragliding ₹1,500–₹2,500.
- **Hadimba Temple**: Ancient wooden temple, free entry, open 8 AM – 6 PM.
- **Old Manali Village**: Quaint cafés and backpacker culture. 3 km from Mall Road.
- **Manu Temple**: On a hilltop near Old Manali village, great views.
- **Beas River**: Rafting from Pirdi to Jhiri, Grade 1–3, ₹600–₹1,200 per person for 14 km.

## Transport

- **Flights**: Bhuntar Airport (KUU), 50 km from Manali. Delhi to Kullu: 1.5h (Air India, SpiceJet), ₹3,500–₹8,000.
- **Bus from Delhi**: Volvo AC bus from ISBT Kashmiri Gate, overnight, 14–16 hours, ₹1,100–₹1,600.
- **Taxi from Bhuntar Airport to Manali**: ₹1,200–₹1,800.
- **Local transport**: 
  - Taxis: ₹1,500–₹2,500 per day for sightseeing.
  - Bikes on rent: ₹600–₹1,200/day (Royal Enfield from Old Manali).

## Travel Tips
- Carry warm clothes even in summer; nights can drop to 5–8°C.
- Altitude sickness possible; rest the first day if coming from sea level.
- Book Rohtang Pass permits online 2 days in advance.
- ATMs available on Mall Road; carry cash for Rohtang Pass area.
- Network connectivity: Airtel and BSNL work best in the valley.
